Design Justification.

These paper prototypes, consitsed of the raw wireframe view of what i wanted the hiddenbehindmist website should look like.
Many small additions to the design, that were not included in the paper prototype, this was a result of constant re-itterating the spacing and postioning of elements.
I wanted to spend time working on smaller elements in my .css stylesheet, like adding :hover animations to headings and texts.

I wanted the design to be simple and straight to the point, showcasing the work of hiddebnbehindmist. This can be seen most, looking at the Gallery.html page. I wanted each page to have different background, still befitting the color pallete.
